Founded in 2020, Eventify is a Sri Lankan-based platform built to revolutionize personal event planning. From intimate weddings to grand surprise parties, we connect you with top-rated vendors and make the entire process delightful, seamless, and stylish.

Our team of event enthusiasts, tech experts, and customer service professionals work tirelessly to ensure your event planning journey is as magical as the event itself.

Our Mission

To simplify event planning by providing a one-stop platform that connects customers with the best vendors, while offering tools to manage every aspect of their event seamlessly.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's most trusted event planning platform, known for innovation, reliability, and creating unforgettable experiences.

Our Values

Customer delight, vendor empowerment, innovation, and integrity guide everything we do at Eventify.
